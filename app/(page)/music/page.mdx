# "vokva"

> "vokva" is an experimental project born out of my struggles with publishing my music.

My goal with this project is to become more comfortable with publishing whatever idea I work on,
no matter how bad it sounds to anyone listening.

<sub>(This is all just a big excuse for me to play with synths and synth vsts more often)</sub>

The name? Its just the word "water" in Icelandic (vÃ¶kva). For whatever reason, I was thinking of ice and water and thus water in *Ice*landic.
